# John Tortes Meyers

John Tortes Meyers was a Major League Baseball catcher for the New York Giants, Boston Braves, and Brooklyn Robins from 1909 to 1917. He maintained a .300 batting average several years and  played in four World Series â€“ the 1911, 1912, and 1913 Series with the Giants, as well as the 1916 Series with the Robins.
